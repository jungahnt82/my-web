WE ARE
ELLIE MEDIA

ELLIE MEDIA is a professional real estate video editing company headquartered in Vietnam, built to serve agents, photographers, and videographers across the globe.

We understand what sells a property — light, movement, and emotion. Our team specializes in transforming raw footage into polished, cinematic property videos that capture attention, build trust, and drive results for your listings.

With over 10,000+ completed projects and 2,000+ satisfied clients worldwide, we are proud to be the trusted post-production partner for creators across the globe.
